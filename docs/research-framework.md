# Research Framework

## Watchlist-first workflow

The system starts with a curated watchlist rather than autonomous market-wide stock picking. Every active idea should have a thesis owner and an explicit review cadence.

## Thesis template

- Summary: one-paragraph explanation of the core thesis.
- Valuation case: why the current price is attractive or unattractive.
- Catalyst window: expected time horizon for thesis realization.
- Key risks: what can go wrong.
- Invalidation rules: the specific conditions that would force a trim, exit, or review.
- Source pack: filings, transcripts, macro notes, and AI-generated summaries.

## AI policy for v1

- AI may summarize filings, news, and transcripts.
- AI may draft research memos and highlight open questions.
- AI may not autonomously issue production buy or sell recommendations.
- Human review is required before anything is promoted into a live watchlist thesis.
